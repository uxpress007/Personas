Persona Library — Tech Ecosystem

Personas in this lane describe people who build, integrate, secure, and scale technology: platform teams, engineering leadership, and cross-functional partners who experience change through APIs, tooling, and operational guardrails rather than only through business process workshops.

Platform & integration lead

Owns reference architectures and paved roads. Cares about deprecation policy, observability defaults, and how product teams consume internal platforms without creating toil.

Security & risk partner

Translates control objectives into workable patterns for squads. Friction shows up when exceptions are opaque or when security reviews arrive late in the cycle.

Developer experience champion

Focused on inner source, docs, and golden paths. Success is measured in reduced time-to-first-successful-build and fewer escalations to infrastructure on-call.
