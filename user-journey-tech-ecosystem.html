User Journey — Tech Ecosystem

Journey maps here focus on how engineers, platform owners, and internal customers move through technical change: discovery of capabilities, onboarding to golden paths, production promotion, and incident learning. Use them alongside the general journey index when the experience is primarily tool- and API-centric.

  • Discover & evaluateCompare internal platforms and external vendors; security review and architecture guardrails shape what is even allowed to progress.
  • Build & integrateLocal development, CI/CD, secrets, and contract testing. Pain often appears as flaky pipelines or unclear ownership of shared libraries.
  • Operate & improveRunbooks, SLOs, post-incident reviews, and roadmap feedback into the platform backlog.
